<p><p>Stone mining and quarrying encompass two primary market types: dimension stone mining and crushed stone mining. Dimension stone mining focuses on extracting large blocks or slabs of stone, such as granite, marble, and limestone, for use in construction, countertops, and building facades. Crushed stone mining, on the other hand, involves breaking down larger stones into smaller aggregates for use in road construction, concrete production, and other construction applications. Both segments cater to the growing demand for durable and aesthetically appealing materials.</p></p>
