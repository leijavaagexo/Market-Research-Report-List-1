<p><p>The stainless steel forgings market comprises several key types: castings, hot/cold forged parts, and sintered parts. Castings involve pouring molten stainless steel into molds, allowing for complex shapes. Hot forged parts are formed at elevated temperatures, enhancing ductility and strength, while cold forged parts are processed at room temperature, preserving dimensional accuracy. Sintered parts result from compacting powdered stainless steel and heating it to create solid forms. Each type serves diverse industrial applications based on specific performance requirements.</p></p>
