<p><strong>The 3D Volumetric Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Volume Display</li><li>Solid-Volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Volumetric Displays market includes two main types: Swept-Volume Displays and Solid-Volume Displays. Swept-Volume Displays create 3D images by moving a projection device or light source through a defined area, offering dynamic visuals with a sense of depth. In contrast, Solid-Volume Displays generate images within a fixed volumetric space, often using technologies like holography or particle manipulation, providing a more stable representation. Both types are utilized across various sectors, enhancing visualization in applications like entertainment, education, and medicine.</p></p>
