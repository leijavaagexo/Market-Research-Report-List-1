<p><p>The Gamma Ray Imager market is segmented into Single Detector and Dual Detector types. Single Detector imagers use one detection unit to capture gamma radiation, providing simplicity and lower costs but limited spatial resolution. In contrast, Dual Detector imagers employ two detection units, allowing for enhanced imaging capabilities, improved sensitivity, and better spatial resolution. The choice between these types depends on the specific application requirements, such as the need for higher accuracy or budget constraints in various professional fields like medical diagnostics and security.</p></p>
