<p><ul><li>Laminated Polypropylene Woven Sacks</li><li>Non-Laminated Polypropylene Woven Sacks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene woven sacks market is divided into two primary types: laminated and non-laminated sacks. Laminated polypropylene woven sacks feature a plastic film coating that enhances moisture resistance, durability, and print quality, making them ideal for storing products sensitive to environmental factors. Non-laminated polypropylene woven sacks, while more economical, offer basic protection and are commonly used for bulk materials like grains or aggregates. Both types cater to various industries, including agriculture, construction, and packaging, providing diverse options based on durability and cost.</p></p>
