<p><strong>The Polyurethane Floor Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Polyurethane Floor Paint</li><li>Non-Solvent Polyurethane Floor Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane floor paint market is divided into two main types: solvent-based and non-solvent-based formulations. Solvent polyurethane floor paint contains organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion and durability, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. However, it may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Non-solvent polyurethane floor paint, often water-based, is more environmentally friendly, with lower VOC emissions. It provides good chemical resistance and is ideal for residential applications, promoting ease of use and clean-up while maintaining a robust finish.</p></p>
