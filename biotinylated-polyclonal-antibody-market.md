<p><p>The biotinylated polyclonal antibody market consists of primary and secondary antibody segments. Primary antibodies bind specifically to target antigens, essential for direct detection in various applications like immunohistochemistry and western blotting. Secondary antibodies, which are conjugated with biotin, bind to primary antibodies, enhancing signal detection and amplification in assays. This dual approach improves sensitivity and specificity in research and diagnostic applications, driving market growth and expanding the utility of biotinylated antibodies in various scientific fields.</p></p>
