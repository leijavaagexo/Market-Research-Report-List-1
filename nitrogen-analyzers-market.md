<p><strong>The Nitrogen Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Method</li><li>Electrode Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen analyzers market features two primary methods: colorimetric and electrode. The colorimetric method involves chemical reactions that produce color changes proportional to nitrogen concentration, enabling quantification through spectrophotometry. In contrast, the electrode method utilizes ion-selective electrodes to measure nitrogen levels based on electrical potential differences. Both approaches serve various industries, including agriculture and wastewater treatment, providing essential data for environmental monitoring, nutrient management, and compliance with regulations, thereby enhancing precision in nitrogen analysis.</p></p>
