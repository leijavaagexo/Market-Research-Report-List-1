<p><p>The laser light cables market is primarily segmented into two types: multimode beam and single-mode beam. Multimode beam cables are designed for short-distance data transmission and can carry multiple light rays or modes simultaneously, making them suitable for local area networks and data centers. In contrast, single-mode beam cables allow only one light mode to travel through, enabling long-distance communication with minimal signal loss, ideal for telecom networks and high-speed internet connections. Both types cater to specific networking needs.</p></p>
