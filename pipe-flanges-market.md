<p><p>The pipe flanges market encompasses various types catering to different application needs. Blind flanges create a seal for closed ends, while lap joint flanges are designed for easy alignment and disassembly. Slip-on flanges allow for simple installation over pipe ends, and socket weld flanges provide strong joints for high-pressure systems by fitting over the pipe. Threaded flanges offer easy installation without welding, making them ideal for applications where welding is impractical. Each type serves distinct requirements in piping systems.</p></p>
