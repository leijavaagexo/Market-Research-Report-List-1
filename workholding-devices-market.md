<p><ul><li>Milling/Drilling Workholding</li><li>Turning Workholding</li><li>Grinding Workholding</li><li>EDM Workholding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The workholding devices market encompasses various types tailored for specific machining processes. Milling and drilling workholding devices secure workpieces during cutting operations, ensuring precision and stability. Turning workholding is designed to hold cylindrical materials firmly on lathes, facilitating accurate machining. Grinding workholding devices provide support and alignment for workpieces during grinding processes to achieve desired surface finishes. EDM workholding is specialized for electrical discharge machining, holding conductive materials securely while allowing for intricate designs and precision tooling.</p></p>
