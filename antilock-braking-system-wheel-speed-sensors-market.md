<p><p>The Antilock Braking System (ABS) wheel speed sensor market includes three primary types: linear wheel speed sensors, ring wheel speed sensors, and Hall type wheel speed sensors. Linear wheel speed sensors use a variable resistance method to detect wheel movement. Ring wheel speed sensors, often magnetic, measure rotations via a toothed ring. Hall type wheel speed sensors utilize magnetic fields to provide precise speed readings. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety by preventing wheel lock-up during braking.</p></p>
