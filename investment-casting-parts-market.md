<p><p>The Investment Casting Parts market comprises various methods, notably the Sodium Silicate Process and the Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/Silica Sol Process. The Sodium Silicate Process utilizes a water-glass binder, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity, making it suitable for large-scale production. In contrast, the Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/Silica Sol Process employs a silica sol binder, which results in finer surface finishes and enhanced dimensional accuracy, ideal for complex geometries and high-precision applications, catering to industries such as aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
