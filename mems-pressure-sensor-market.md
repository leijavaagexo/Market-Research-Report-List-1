<p><strong>The MEMS Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Pressure Sensors</li><li>Piezoresistive Pressure Sensors</li><li>Piezoelectric Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS pressure sensor market includes three primary types: capacitive, piezoresistive, and piezoelectric sensors. Capacitive pressure sensors operate by detecting changes in capacitance caused by pressure variations, offering high sensitivity and stability. Piezoresistive sensors leverage changes in electrical resistance due to mechanical stress, providing accurate pressure measurements in compact designs. Piezoelectric sensors generate an electric charge in response to applied pressure, ideal for dynamic pressure monitoring. Each type serves various applications, including automotive, industrial, and healthcare sectors.</p></p>
