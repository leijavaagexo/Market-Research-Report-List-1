<p><p>The Portable Near Infrared Diffuse Reflectance Analyzer market encompasses two main types: Raster Scan Spectroscopy and Fourier Spectroscopy. Raster Scan Spectroscopy captures spectral data by scanning a sample in a grid pattern, allowing for detailed spatial analysis. In contrast, Fourier Spectroscopy utilizes interferometry to obtain high-resolution spectral data rapidly, providing comprehensive chemical composition insights. Both techniques enhance material characterization across industries such as agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and food safety, offering portability and ease of use in various analytical applications.</p></p>
