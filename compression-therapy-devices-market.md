<p><strong>The Compression Therapy Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Compression Therapy</li><li>Dynamic Compression Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compression therapy devices are classified into two main types: static and dynamic compression therapy. Static compression therapy involves the application of consistent pressure to a specific area, typically using bandages or stockings, to improve circulation and reduce swelling. In contrast, dynamic compression therapy employs adjustable mechanisms like pneumatic devices that intermittently inflate and deflate, enhancing blood flow and promoting lymphatic drainage. Both types aim to alleviate conditions such as edema and venous disorders, optimizing patient recovery and comfort.</p></p>
