<p><p>The Semiconductor Wafer Defect Inspection System market encompasses various technologies, including Electron Beam Inspection (EBI), Bright-Field Inspection, and Dark-Field Inspection systems. EBI utilizes focused electron beams to detect critical defects with high resolution, making it suitable for advanced nodes. Bright-Field Inspection employs transmitted light to identify defects on wafers by highlighting variations in transparency, while Dark-Field Inspection utilizes scattered light to reveal defects that would otherwise be invisible. Each method serves specific applications in ensuring semiconductor manufacturing quality.</p></p>
