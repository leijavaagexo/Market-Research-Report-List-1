<p><ul><li>Ready to Eat (RTE)</li><li>Ready to Heat (RTH)</li><li>Ready to Cook (RTC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prepared vegetables market comprises three main types: Ready to Eat (RTE), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Cook (RTC). RTE vegetables are fully cooked and require no preparation, offering convenience for immediate consumption. RTH vegetables are partially cooked and need only heating before serving, providing a quick meal option. RTC vegetables, on the other hand, are pre-cut or prepped but require cooking by the consumer, catering to those seeking a fresh cooking experience without extensive prep work.</p></p>
