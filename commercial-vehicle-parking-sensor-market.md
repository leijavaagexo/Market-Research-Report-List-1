<p><p>The commercial vehicle parking sensor market is categorized into two main types: forward and rear-view sensors. Forward sensors are designed to detect obstacles in the path of the vehicle while moving forward, enhancing safety during maneuvers in tight spaces. Rear-view sensors, on the other hand, focus on obstacles behind the vehicle, assisting drivers when reversing. Both types utilize ultrasonic or radar technology to provide audio or visual alerts, improving overall safety and efficiency in commercial vehicle operations.</p></p>
