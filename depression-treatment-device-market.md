<p><p>The Depression Treatment Device Market encompasses various neuromodulation technologies aimed at alleviating depressive symptoms. Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation (tDCS) uses low electrical currents to modulate neuronal activity. Transcranial Alternating Current Stimulation (tACS) applies alternating currents to influence brain rhythms. Transcranial Pulsed Current Stimulation (tPCS) delivers pulsed currents for targeted stimulation. Transcranial Random Noise Stimulation (tRNS) employs random noise to enhance cortical excitability. Electroconvulsive Therapy (ECT) is a more intensive method involving electrical induction of seizures. Each device offers distinct mechanisms for therapeutic intervention in depression.</p></p>
