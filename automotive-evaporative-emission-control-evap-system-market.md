<p><p>The Automotive Evaporative Emission Control (EVAP) System market comprises various components essential for controlling fuel vapors. The fuel tank stores gasoline and minimizes vapor release. The gas cap seals the tank, preventing emissions. The liquid vapor separator separates liquid fuel from vapor. The canister stores fuel vapors until they are purged. Control valves manage the flow of vapors, while sensors monitor the system's performance. Hoses connect these components, facilitating the effective containment and management of harmful emissions.</p></p>
