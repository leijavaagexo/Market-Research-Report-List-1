<p><p>The pet multivitamins market comprises two primary types: fat-soluble and water-soluble vitamins. Fat-soluble vitamins, including A, D, E, and K, can be stored in the body’s fatty tissues and liver, making them essential for long-term health benefits such as maintaining vision, bone strength, and immune function. Conversely, water-soluble vitamins, such as B-complex and C, are not stored and require regular intake to support energy metabolism, nervous system health, and overall vitality in pets.</p></p>
