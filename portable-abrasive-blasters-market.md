<p><ul><li>Pressure</li><li>Siphon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable abrasive blaster market is primarily divided into two types: pressure and siphon blasters. Pressure blasters operate by pressurizing the abrasive material, allowing for a more forceful blast and faster cleaning or finishing. They are often preferred for heavy-duty applications. Siphon blasters, on the other hand, use a siphoning method to draw abrasive from a container, resulting in a gentler application. This makes them suitable for lighter tasks or detailed work, providing versatility for various cleaning and surface preparation needs.</p></p>
