<p><p>Noise Monitoring Stations are categorized into two main types: Portable Noise Monitoring Systems and Permanent Noise Monitoring Systems. Portable systems are designed for flexibility, allowing users to measure noise levels in various locations temporarily, making them ideal for assessing noise impact during construction or events. In contrast, Permanent Noise Monitoring Systems are installed in fixed locations for continuous monitoring, providing long-term data collection to manage noise pollution in urban areas, industrial sites, or airports, ensuring compliance with regulations and public health standards.</p></p>
