<p><strong>The Programmatic Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Real Time Bidding (RTB)</li><li>Programmatic Direct</li><li>Private Exchange Buying (PMP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Programmatic display advertising encompasses various market types. Real-Time Bidding (RTB) enables advertisers to bid on ad impressions in real time via an auction system, allowing for dynamic pricing and targeting. Programmatic Direct involves fixed-price transactions between advertisers and publishers without an auction, ensuring guaranteed inventory. Private Exchange Buying (PMP) combines elements of both, as it offers curated access to premium inventory in a private marketplace, allowing advertisers to bid on high-quality ad spaces while maintaining exclusivity.</p></p>
