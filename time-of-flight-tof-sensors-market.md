<p><p>Time of Flight (ToF) sensors are categorized into two main types: Direct ToF and Indirect ToF sensors. </p><p>Direct ToF sensors measure the time it takes for emitted light to travel to a target and back, providing precise distance data in real-time. Conversely, Indirect ToF sensors utilize phase shift or modulation techniques to derive distance, typically resulting in lower accuracy but allowing for longer ranges and more cost-effective solutions. Each type serves distinct applications in fields like automotive, robotics, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
