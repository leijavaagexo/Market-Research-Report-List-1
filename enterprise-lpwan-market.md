<p><strong>The Enterprise LPWAN Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellular Based LPWAN Technologies</li><li>Non-Cellular LPWAN Technologies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Enterprise LPWAN market is divided into two main types: Cellular-based and Non-cellular LPWAN technologies. Cellular-based LPWAN technologies utilize existing cellular networks and standards, offering wide coverage and reliable connectivity for IoT applications, such as NB-IoT and LTE-M. In contrast, Non-cellular LPWAN technologies, like LoRa and Sigfox, rely on unlicensed spectrum to provide low-power, long-range connectivity, often in urban or rural areas where cellular networks may be less efficient or cost-effective for specific use cases.</p></p>
