<p><p>The microphone market primarily features two major types: dynamic microphones and condenser microphones. Dynamic microphones are robust, with a simple design, making them ideal for live performances and high-SPL environments. They excel in durability and feedback rejection. In contrast, condenser microphones are more sensitive and offer a broader frequency response, making them suitable for studio recordings and capturing subtle audio details. Each type caters to specific needs, influencing their applications in music, broadcasting, and audiovisual productions.</p></p>
