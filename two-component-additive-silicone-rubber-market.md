Self-adhesive silicone rubber bonds effectively to various substrates without the need for additional adhesives, making it ideal for applications requiring strong adhesion. In contrast, non-self-adhesive silicone rubber requires external bonding agents, offering flexibility in applications that may not require immediate adhesion.
