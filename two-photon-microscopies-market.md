<p><p>The two-photon microscopy market includes various types of imaging systems. Fluorescence microscopes utilize fluorescent markers to visualize specimens, while widefield microscopes provide a broad view but may have limitations in resolution. Confocal microscopes enhance clarity and detail using pinholes to block out-of-focus light. In contrast, two-photon microscopes excel in deep tissue imaging, minimizing photodamage and enabling clearer images in thick samples. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to diverse applications in biological and medical research.</p></p>
