<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade bentonite market primarily consists of two types: sodium bentonite and calcium bentonite. Sodium bentonite is known for its superior swelling properties, making it ideal for use in drug formulation and as an excipient. It can absorb water and other materials effectively. In contrast, calcium bentonite exhibits lower swelling capacity but is valued for its ability to enhance stability in formulations. Both types play crucial roles in the pharmaceutical industry, contributing to drug efficacy and safety.</p></p>
